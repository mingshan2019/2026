Welcome to HIS 2026

The 15th International Conference on Health Information Science (HIS 2026) provides a forum for disseminating and exchanging multidisciplinary research results in computer science/information technology and health science & services. HIS 2026 aims to integrate computer science/information technology with health sciences & services, embracing information science research coupled with topics related to modelling, design, development, integration, and management of health information systems and health services.
